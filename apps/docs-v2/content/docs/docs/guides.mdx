## What's Included

### User Installation Guide

Learn how to install SkyHelper directly to your Discord account, enabling you to use commands anywhere on Discord - in DMs, group chats, and servers where the bot isn't installed.

### Linked Role Connection

Discover how to showcase your Sky: Children of the Light profile on your Discord profile using Discord's linked role feature with SkyHelper.

### Translation Contributions

Join our community translation effort to make SkyHelper available in multiple languages. No technical knowledge required - just help translate text through our user-friendly platform.

### Community Recognition

See who's behind SkyHelper's success - from shard predictors to guide creators, translators to beta testers.
